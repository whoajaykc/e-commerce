Who We Are?

Embark on a unique journey with us! We are a passionate students hailing from CMR Institute of Technology (CMRIT) in Bangalore, venturing into the captivating world of custom-made anime mobile covers. Fueled by our love for anime and driven by entrepreneurial spirit, we are dedicated to introducing and spreading the vibrant culture of anime across India. Our mission goes beyond business; it's about celebrating creativity and individuality through our personalized anime-themed phone covers. With an unwavering commitment to quality and innovation, we aim to bring a touch of anime artistry into the everyday lives of fellow enthusiasts. Join us as we blend our passion for anime with the art of customization, creating unique and expressive mobile covers that resonate with the anime community in India.



Follow us on Instagram @comic_cases.xyz to discover exclusive designs, behind-the-scenes moments, and exciting updates.
